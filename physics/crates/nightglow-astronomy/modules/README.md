# Astronomy module index

These directories isolate distinct sources of astrometric error: clocks, reference frames, Earth orientation, solar-system ephemerides, stellar motion, and catalogue LOD. Their outputs are assembled by `nightglow-astronomy` and consumed by the solver and physical source modules.

Research and external standards are summarized in [Astronomy and time](../../../docs/research/astronomy-and-time.md) and [Stars and catalogues](../../../docs/research/stars-and-catalogs.md).
